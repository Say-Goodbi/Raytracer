Detailed Description

Implementation of the RayTracer::Camera renderer.

Implements ray generation and deterministic path-tracing for the camera renderer. The camera builds a view plane from a Geometry::TransformMatrix and exposes generateRay() and render() used by the scene writer. See RayTracer::Camera and Geometry::Ray for related types.
