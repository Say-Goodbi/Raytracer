12 /**
13 * @class Camera
14 * @brief Renderer that evaluates the rendering equation deterministically.
15 *
16 * This camera uses the Monte Carlo path-tracing framework (BRDF evaluation,
17 * PDF weighting, recursive bounces) but removes all randomness:
18 *
19 * - **Bounce directions** are chosen deterministically by each material's
20 * `IMaterial::sample()` (e.g. perfect specular reflection, cosine-lobe
21 * centroid) instead of being drawn from a random distribution.
22 * - **Anti-aliasing** is done with a fixed stratified sub-pixel grid
23 * (`_sqrtSamples × _sqrtSamples` evenly-spaced samples) rather than
24 * jittered or random samples.
25 *
26 * The result is fully reproducible: identical inputs always produce
27 * identical output, with no noise and no need for a random seed.
28 */

30 {
31 private:
32 /// Camera world-space position.

41 int _maxDepth = 8;
