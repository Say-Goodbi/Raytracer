14 /**
15 * @class Camera
16 * @brief Renderer that evaluates the rendering equation deterministically.
17 *
18 * This camera uses the Monte Carlo path-tracing framework (BRDF evaluation,
19 * PDF weighting, recursive bounces) but removes all randomness:
20 *
21 * - **Bounce directions** are chosen deterministically by each material's
22 * `IMaterial::sample()` (e.g. perfect specular reflection, cosine-lobe
23 * centroid) instead of being drawn from a random distribution.
24 * - **Anti-aliasing** is done with a fixed stratified sub-pixel grid
25 * (`_sqrtSamples × _sqrtSamples` evenly-spaced samples) rather than
26 * jittered or random samples.
27 *
28 * The result is fully reproducible: identical inputs always produce
29 * identical output, with no noise and no need for a random seed.
30 */
