22 /**
23 * @class BVHCam
24 * @brief Renderer that evaluates the rendering equation deterministically.
25 *
26 * This camera uses the Monte Carlo path-tracing framework (BRDF evaluation,
27 * PDF weighting, recursive bounces) but removes all randomness:
28 *
29 * - **Bounce directions** are chosen deterministically by each material's
30 * `IMaterial::sample()` (e.g. perfect specular reflection, cosine-lobe
31 * centroid) instead of being drawn from a random distribution.
32 * - **Anti-aliasing** is done with a fixed stratified sub-pixel grid
33 * (`_sqrtSamples × _sqrtSamples` evenly-spaced samples) rather than
34 * jittered or random samples.
35 *
36 * The result is fully reproducible: identical inputs always produce
37 * identical output, with no noise and no need for a random seed.
38 */
